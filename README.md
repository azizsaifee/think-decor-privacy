# Privacy Policy for Think Decor

**Last updated:** May 2026

**Bharmal Tech** ("we," "our," or "us") operates the **Think Decor** mobile application (the "App") for iOS. We are deeply committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring a transparent user experience. 

This Privacy Policy explains how we handle data and information when you use our App. By downloading and using Think Decor, you agree to the terms outlined below.

---

## 1. Information We Collect and Process

### 🎥 Camera and AR Data
Think Decor utilizes Apple’s advanced **ARKit** frameworks to provide its core functionality—allowing you to preview 3D furniture models and receive room redecoration suggestions directly in your physical environment. 
* To make this possible, the App requires access to your device’s camera to detect surfaces (like floors and walls) and calculate spatial dimensions.
* **On-Device Processing:** All camera data, environmental spatial maps, and visual feeds are processed **strictly in real-time, locally on your physical device**. 
* **No External Transmission:** We do not collect, store, capture, or transmit any video feeds, photos, or spatial mapping data to external servers, cloud services, or third parties.

### 🖼️ Photos and Media Library
If you choose to use the feature that captures your AR room setups to save or share them:
* The App will request permission to write to your iOS Photo Library.
* This access is strictly handled through native iOS APIs to save the generated image file. We do not have access to, read, or upload your existing photos or media.

### 👤 Personal Data & Accounts
* **No Registration Required:** Think Decor does not require a user account, registration, login, or any form of profile creation.
* **No Identity Tracking:** We do not collect, request, or store any personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, phone number, billing information, or precise GPS location.

---

## 2. Third-Party Services and Analytics
* **No Third-Party SDKs:** Think Decor does not integrate third-party analytics platforms, data brokers, tracking SDKs, or advertising networks. Your behavior within the app is completely private and is not tracked across other websites or apps.

---

## 3. Data Security
Because all data processing happens locally on your physical device, your architectural scans and camera feeds are inherently protected by Apple’s sandboxed security architecture. No cloud databases are maintained where your personal environment data could be breached.

---

## 4. Children’s Privacy
Our App is safe for all audiences. Because we do not collect, harvest, or request any information from any user, we do not knowingly or unknowingly collect data from children under the age of 13.
